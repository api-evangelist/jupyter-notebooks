The Jupyter Executive Council and Jupyter Foundation are pleased to announce a new Call for Proposals (CFP) for funding the Jupyter community to improve Jupyter. Visit the Jupyter Foundation Community Proposals webpage to learn more about the process and how to submit proposals. Submit a proposal by Wednesday, September 9, 2026.
